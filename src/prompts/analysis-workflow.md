### Phase 3: Technical Analysis

**3A. Moving Averages** — 8/20/50/200-day SMA vs price. Golden/death cross?

**3B. Momentum** — RSI (14), MACD, MFI with interpretations.

**3C. Key Levels** — strong support, MA support/resistance, strong resistance, 52w extremes.

**3D. Price Action** — 1W/1M/3M/6M/1Y % changes.

**3E. Summary** — uptrend/downtrend/consolidation in one paragraph.

**4A. Consensus** — rating, avg/high/low target, # analysts.

**4B. Individual analysts** — firm, analyst, rating, target, date.

**4C. Options sentiment** (if depth=deep) — put/call OI ratio, IV, IV skew.

### Phase 5: Risk Assessment

- **Company risks**: debt, cash burn, dilution, execution, legal
- **Industry risks**: competition, cyclicality, disruption, geopolitical
- **Stock risks**: liquidity, volatility (Beta), short interest, insider selling

### Phase 7: Investment & Trading Advice

**7A. Scenarios** — bull/base/bear/worst with probability, 12M target, triggers. Compute probability-weighted price.

**7B. Position sizing** — by investor profile (conservative/moderate/aggressive).

**7C. Entry** — 3-4 batch tranches with price triggers.

**7D. Exit** — take-profit levels, hard stop-loss, trailing stop %.

**7E. Existing holders** — advice by P&L status (profit / small loss / large loss).

**7F. Short-term traders** — range trading levels, breakout triggers, catalyst calendar.

**7G. Alternatives** — 2-3 lower-risk sector peers if stock is high-risk.
