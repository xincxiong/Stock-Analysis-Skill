## Scoring Rubric (1-10 per dimension)

### Financial Health (20%)
- 9-10: Net cash, D/E < 0.3, current ratio > 3
- 7-8: D/E < 1.0, current ratio > 2
- 5-6: D/E 1-2, adequate liquidity
- 3-4: D/E 2-4, tight liquidity
- 1-2: D/E > 4, distressed, bankruptcy risk

### Profitability (15%)
- 9-10: Net margin > 20%, expanding, strong FCF
- 7-8: Net margin 10-20%, positive FCF
- 5-6: Net margin 0-10%
- 3-4: Negative but improving
- 1-2: Deep losses, no path to profit

### Growth Potential (15%)
- 9-10: Revenue > 30% YoY, massive TAM
- 7-8: 15-30% growth, multiple vectors
- 5-6: 5-15% growth
- 3-4: Flat or slight decline
- 1-2: Declining > 10%, losing share

### Competitive Moat (15%)
- 9-10: Dominant position, strong IP, network effects
- 7-8: Clear tech/brand lead, barriers
- 5-6: Some differentiation, intensifying competition
- 3-4: Commoditized, low barriers
- 1-2: No moat, price competition

### Technical Setup (10%)
- 9-10: Above all key MAs, golden cross, RSI 50-65
- 7-8: Above 200 SMA, bullish MACD
- 5-6: Mixed, between key MAs
- 3-4: Below 200 SMA, bearish MACD
- 1-2: Death cross, breakdown

### Valuation (10%)
- 9-10: Significantly undervalued vs peers (P/E, P/B, P/S, DCF)
- 7-8: Moderately undervalued
- 5-6: Fairly valued
- 3-4: Overvalued or value trap
- 1-2: Significantly overvalued

### Industry Outlook (10%)
- 9-10: Secular megatrend (AI, EV), strong tailwinds
- 7-8: Growing, favorable trends
- 5-6: Stable/mature
- 3-4: Cyclical downturn or headwinds
- 1-2: Declining, disruption risk

### Risk Management (5%)
- 9-10: Beta < 1, diversified, clean governance
- 7-8: Beta 1-1.5, manageable
- 5-6: Beta 1.5-2
- 3-4: Beta 2-3, dilution, governance concerns
- 1-2: Beta > 3, active dilution, fraud flags

## Position Sizing
- Score 8-10: up to 8-10% portfolio
- Score 6-8: up to 5-7%
- Score 4-6: up to 2-4%
- Score 2-4: up to 1-2%
- Score 0-2: avoid

## Stop-Loss Guide
- Large cap, low beta: -8% to -12%
- Mid cap: -12% to -18%
- Small cap, high beta: -18% to -25%
- Speculative / turnaround: -25% to -35%
